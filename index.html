Defend My Food

Instructions

Prevent the Naruto shadow clones from getting Gokus ramen noodles!

  1. Add Gokus for $250 with a single click anywhere on the board to protect the ramen!

  2. Be clever about where you place them! If a Naruto hits you, you will lose life force!

  3. Destroy a Naruto and get $100

  4. Gokus Kamehameha waves shoot in a "randomized" order, bouncing in midair! If you figure out the pattern contact the creator for a prize (no cheating on the github)!

  5. With a little luck at the beginning, this should be smooth sailing. Good Luck!
