Defend My Food

Instructions

Prevent the Naruto shadow clones from getting Gokus ramen noodles!

  1. Drag (single click on a phone) Gokus for $250 onto the board to protect the ramen!

  2. Start the wave of Narutos by adding your first goku!

  3. Be clever about where you place them! If a Naruto hits you, you will lose money!

  4. Destroy a Naruto and get $100

  5. Goku shoots kamehameha waves in a "randomized" order, bouncing in midair! If you figure out the pattern contact the creator for a prize (no cheating on the github)!

  6. The game increases in difficulty as your Kill Count increases. See if you can survive after 150 kills!
